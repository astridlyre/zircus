faq

What are your shipping rates?
  • Standard - $4.99 USD (5-7 business days)
  • Express - $24.99 USD (2-3 business days)
  • Next Day - $35.00 USD (1-2 business days)

Orders over over $150 USD are eligible for free shipping.

What are your payment methods?

Visa, Visa Debit, Mastercard, American Express (Canada Only), Shop Pay, PayPal, Afterpay, Google Pay, and Apple Pay.

Do you ship internationally?

Zircus ships to Canada and the United States. We hope to expand to international shipping soon!

What is the processing time of each order?

All orders placed Monday through Friday will begin processing the following business day. Please note that orders placed on Friday will begin processing on the following Monday. Zircus will ship within 2-4 business days after processing has begun. Please expect 2-7 business days for shipping to arrive within Canada and 3-6 business days for shipping to the US. Delivery times do not include Saturday, Sunday or Holidays.

What is your return policy?

Unfortunately, due to our minimal packaging we cannot accept returns since there is no way to ensure the underwear has not been worn.

I just placed an order online but have not received an email confirmation yet, has by order been received?

You should receive an email confirming your order within 10-15 minutes of placing an order online. In the unlikely case you have not received a confirmation please email support@zircus.com.

Can I change or cancel my order once placed?

As we aim to ship your order as quickly as possible, we regret that it is usually not possible for us to handle change or cancel requests once an order has been submitted. Please ensure that you have verified your order details prior to submission, especially the billing and shipping address. Zircus can be contacted during business hours but if we are unable to facilitate an order change or cancellation request, you may process a return under our Comfort Guarantee policy.

How do I know when my order has shipped?

An email will be sent to you once your order has shipped and will include your tracking number. Please note, the package may not be trackable until the shipment has been picked up and scanned by the carrier.

I mistakenly ordered the wrong item, what should I do?

Please contact us at email support@zircus.com as soon as possible.

What if my order is lost or damaged in the mail?

Zircus is not liable for any damages incurred during transit; it lies within the responsibility of the local carrier. All purchases are made at the buyers’ own risk. Zircus does not assume any responsibility for lost or damaged packages.

My tracking information shows as "delivered" but I don't have the package and no notice has been left at my residence, what should I do?

Please contact your local carrier’s office to see if a parcel has been left for you; In certain cases, a parcel will be left with a local carrier’s office if the recipient is not available upon delivery. If you have not been able to locate the parcel after doing this, please email support@zircus.com with your order number and confirmed shipping address.

How do I wash my Zircus underwear?

Most garments can be machine washed with cold water and tumble dried with low heat on a gentle cycle. Note: only iron (low heat), bleach (using non-chlorine bleach), or dry clean when needed. These are general washing instructions and individual product pages/labels/packaging should be referenced for each style as washing instructions may vary slightly.
